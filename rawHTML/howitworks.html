How it works

Play Video
The way to winning a Ford EcoSport. Or a 2014 Ford Fiesta.

1. Choose a Celeb

Pick a celeb to teach. Or choose to teach all three.

2. Make an appeal

Give your celeb a reason to choose you. Tell them what you can teach them in their chosen field. Boast a bit.

3. Ace the audition (Round 1)

If you are one of the chosen few, this city-wise audition will be your first test. Prove your boast was true.

4. Wow the celeb (Round 2)

If shortlisted, you’ll face another audition. This time with the celebs as judges. Prove your worth. Wow the celebs.

5. Drive and Teach (Round 3)

Drive off with your celeb. On the way, you will need to train him/her for the task undertaken. It’s just this team now. And mobile apps on SYNC® AppLinkTM.

6. Get friends talking

A video of your task will be posted on the site. Get your social media pages buzzing. Post, tweet, instagram . Gather ‘Votes’ from your friends and peers to get ahead.

7. The final word

A panel of judges and the votes collected by your video will give the final verdict. Keep your fingers crossed.

8. Choose your win

The Ford EcoSport or the 2014 Ford Fiesta.
